Welcome to Trimex Library

Trimex Library focuses on 3 core values

Intellectual Freedom

Libraries champion the free exchange of ideas, ensuring access to diverse, unbiased information. This commitment to neutrality is a cornerstone of democratic knowledge sharing.

Lifelong Learning

As institutions that have historically supported education, libraries foster continuous personal and professional development. They curate resources and programs that honor academic traditions while propelling future self-improvement.

Community Engagement

At the heart of every library is a commitment to serve as a community hub—facilitating access, hosting collaborative initiatives, and encouraging civic participation to integrate collective wisdom with modern demands.

About Trimex Library

Trimex Library offers a rich collection of resources and a welcoming environment for learning and growth.

Our Mission

The library commits itself to the optimum development of the persons in the community, provide effective materials for instruction, pursue research and undertake responsive services.

Our Vision

The library envision itself a wide range of information, resources and services to all personnel, faculty, students and to the academic community for the transformation of individuals and Christ- centered steward of the community.
